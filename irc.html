irc and you

now what can it do?

So you were probably directed here after someone kept bugging you to join the IRC channel or you've heard someone mention IRC and are curious what it is. At the moment, this guide is geared to the former because it is getting late and I have papers to write for class after this.

IRC is a protocol for chat. With an IRC client, you log into a server where you can chat with other users on that server. Although you can message people privately, people typically chat in channels.
